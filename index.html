About Us

Nestled in the heart of the city, The Jaffna Bounty opened its doors in early 2025 with a bold vision: to bring the rich and vibrant flavors of Northern Sri Lanka to a wider audience. Founded by culinary enthusiast and Jaffna-native, Kumar Sivaguru, the restaurant was born from a deep desire to honor tradition while offering a contemporary dining experience. Growing up in Jaffna, Kumar was immersed in the aromas of slow-cooked crab curries, fragrant mutton poriyals, and spicy dosai sizzling on cast-iron griddles. After moving abroad and spending over a decade in the hospitality industry, he dreamt of creating a space where people could not just eat, but feel the soul of Jaffna cuisine. The name “The Jaffna Bounty” reflects both the richness of the region’s culinary heritage and the generous spirit of its people. Every dish is crafted with hand-ground spices, locally-sourced ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations; some even handwritten in old Tamil script. Since its opening, The Jaffna Bounty has quickly gained attention for its authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and minimalist yet earthy decor inspired by the palmyra trees and red soil of northern Sri Lanka.
